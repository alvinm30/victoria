Victoria Quant Lab was created to bridge the gap between academic quantitative research and practical algorithmic trading. The goal is to share tools, indicators, and strategies that are grounded in rigorous analysis — not hype. Every indicator is backed by research. Every EA is tested with data. This lab serves as a platform for transparency, education, and collaboration in quantitative finance.
