About This Indicator

INDICATOR PLATFORM: TradingView  |  VERSION: 1.0  |  LANGUAGE: Pine Script  |  AUTHOR: Victoria
smc-indicator.overview

This indicator encodes Smart Money Concepts (SMC) methodology directly onto the TradingView chart. It is designed to help traders identify where institutional money is likely positioned — without discretionary guesswork. Every element plotted has a defined rule-based condition.

Built from actual trading use — not theoretical. The logic mirrors how I personally read market structure: top-down bias from higher timeframes, then precision entries at lower timeframe confirmations using Order Blocks and Fair Value Gaps.

What It Plots

Break of Structure (BOS)

Detects when price breaks a significant swing high or low, confirming a continuation of the current market structure direction.

Change of Character (CHoCH)

Identifies the first sign of a structural reversal — the shift from bullish to bearish structure or vice versa before BOS confirmation.

Order Blocks (OB)

Marks the last up/down candle before a strong impulsive move. These zones represent areas where institutional orders were likely placed.

Fair Value Gaps (FVG)

Highlights three-candle imbalances where price moved too fast, leaving unfilled gaps that price tends to return to fill.

Liquidity Levels

Identifies equal highs and equal lows where stop losses are clustered — areas where institutional players tend to hunt liquidity before moving.

Configurable Settings

Toggle each element on/off independently. Adjust lookback periods, color themes, and label visibility to suit your chart setup.

How to Use

usage-guide.md
> step 1: Open TradingView and search "Victoria SMC Concept Indicator" in the indicator library.
> step 2: Add to chart. Use on a higher timeframe (H4/Daily) first to establish bias.
> step 3: Drop to lower timeframe (M15/H1) to find CHoCH/BOS confirmation for entry.
> step 4: Use Order Blocks and FVGs as entry zones. Set stop below/above the OB.
> step 5: Target the next liquidity pool (equal highs/lows) for take profit.
